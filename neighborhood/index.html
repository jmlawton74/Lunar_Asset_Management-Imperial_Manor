Location

West Detroit convenience near shopping and dining

Imperial Manor sits on Appleton Street on Detroit's west side, close to everyday errands, local food, and west-side recreation. Nearby options include Demi's Restaurant & Bar, Lola Valley Park, Bell Creek Recreation Area, and the Detroit Zoo, while many popular Detroit destinations are a 10- to 25-minute drive toward Corktown, Midtown, Downtown, Royal Oak, or Ferndale.
